Our Mission

Every Student Should Know What They Want

Every student should complete: "I want to live a life where I solve _____ because _____"

We don't just teach careers. We develop students who take initiative, identify problems worth solving, and believe they can shape their own futures.

"Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself." - John Dewey

About RETEC

Founded to address the crisis of directionless graduates in Ghana. Built by educators who experienced the system's limitations and created what was missing - a curriculum that develops initiative and clear life direction in students.
